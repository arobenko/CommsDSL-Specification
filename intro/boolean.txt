[[intro-boolean]]
=== Boolean Values ===
There are properties that require boolean value. The **CommsDSL** supports 
case **insensitive** "true" and "false" strings, as well as "1" and "0" numeric
values.
[source,xml]
----
<int name="SomeField" ... removed="True" />
<int name="SomeOtherField" ... pseudo="0" />
----
